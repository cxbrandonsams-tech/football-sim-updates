# Roadmap

Planned features and future direction for Football Sim. Items are roughly in priority order but subject to change.

---

## Recently Completed

### Team + GM Navigation Merge (2026-04-13) — SHIPPED
Merged Team and GM into a single section with dual-lens roster (Performance/Contracts toggle), absorbed Trades and Free Agents as Team sub-tabs.

### Hall of Fame & Ring of Honor Recalibration (2026-04-13) — SHIPPED
Raised thresholds, added waiting periods, capped yearly inductees, required minimum tenure for ROH.

### Primetime Design System (2026-04-12) — SHIPPED
Monochrome + 6-accent color system (neon, cyan, steel, gold, earth, football). Space Grotesk/Manrope/Inter typography. Glass panels with card depth. Page entrance choreography.

### Prose Engine + Radio Show (2026-04-12) — SHIPPED
Data-driven prose engine replacing templates. "The Brandon Sams Show" AI-generated sports radio with OpenAI TTS, caller personas, and auto-scrolling transcript UI.

### Almanac Expansion to 13 Tabs (2026-04-12) — SHIPPED
Ring of Honor, GM Career, History, and Hall of Fame moved into Almanac. Full-bleed glass + backdrop layout across all tabs.

### League Restructure + ELO (2026-04-12) — SHIPPED
New Rankings page with tiered power rankings + ELO. Team Stats split (Offensive/Defensive/Efficiency). News redesigned as "The Gridiron Chronicle" newspaper layout. 8 new defensive/efficiency stats.

### SVG Player Portraits (2026-04-11) — SHIPPED
Procedurally generated 11-layer SVG faces with seeded RNG. Appear across all views.

### Confidence-Based Scouting (2026-04-11) — SHIPPED
4-stage pipeline replacing point-based scouting. Pre-rolled 18-week college season. Full Stitch UI.

### Stitch Design System + Team Reskin (2026-04-08) — SHIPPED
8-primitive component kit. Team section fully reskinned. Depth chart redesign. Playbook editor. Waiver wire + trade block.

### Almanac (2026-04-05) — SHIPPED
9-tab historical reference hub (now expanded to 13).

### Narrative & Immersion Systems (2026-04-05) — SHIPPED, LOCKED
Record-chase detection, extended milestones, rivalry system, narrative arcs.
