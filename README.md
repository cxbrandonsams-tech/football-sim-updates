# Football Sim

A premium football management simulation where you build, manage, and lead a franchise to glory.

## What Is This?

Football Sim is a deep football management game featuring:

- **Full franchise management** - roster building, contracts, dead cap, fifth-year options, trades, free agency, waivers, coaching, scouting, and the draft
- **Realistic simulation engine** - play-by-play game simulation with explicit pass/run phase resolution, penalties, overtime, two-minute drills, and YAC
- **2D animated game visualization** - top-down view of all 22 players running routes, covering, blocking, and tackling in real-time with commentary synced to the action
- **Rich broadcast experience** - play-by-play commentary with phrase fragments, momentum tracking, drive narratives, and atmosphere text
- **Deep strategy layer** - custom offensive and defensive playbooks, gameplans, formation depth charts, and playbook-fit scoring
- **Complete season lifecycle** - regular season, playoffs, offseason week windows, draft, progression/regression, Hall of Fame, and Ring of Honor
- **Primetime design system** - monochrome plus accent palette, glass panels, typography tuned for dense sports UI, and page entrance choreography
- **Almanac** - Hall of Fame, Ring of Honor, Records, Season Recaps, Head to Head, Replays, and GM Career
- **Confidence-based scouting** - 4-stage pipeline with a pre-rolled 18-week college season across 50 schools and 5 conferences
- **Prose engine** - data-driven sports writing with vocabulary banks, context tagging, and variety tracking
- **The Brandon Sams Show** - AI-generated sports radio with voices, caller personas, and an auto-scrolling transcript sidebar
- **Narrative & immersion** - record-chase alerts, rivalry heat, career milestones, and multi-week story arcs
- **SVG player portraits** - procedurally generated seeded faces for every player, prospect, and Hall of Famer
- **ELO ratings & power rankings** - margin-of-victory adjusted ELO with tiered power rankings
- **Live dashboard** - team overview, standings, playoff race, team leaders, weekly recap, injury report, upcoming games, recent schedule
- **Multiplayer** - multi-user leagues with per-game ready-up, live spectating, and commissioner tools

## Tech Stack

- React 19 + TypeScript + Vite 8
- Tailwind CSS v4 with Primetime design system
- Express API + SQLite backend
- Framer Motion animations
- OpenAI TTS + Cloudflare R2 (radio show)
- Deployed on Vercel (frontend) + Fly.io (backend)
