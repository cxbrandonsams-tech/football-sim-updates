# Football Sim

A premium football management simulation where you build, manage, and lead a franchise to glory.

## What Is This?

Football Sim is a deep football management game featuring:

- **Full franchise management** — roster building, contracts, trades, free agency, scouting, and the draft
- **Realistic simulation engine** — play-by-play game simulation with pass/run phase resolution, penalties, overtime, two-minute drills, and 125+ plays per game
- **2D animated game visualization** — top-down view of all 22 players running routes, covering, blocking, and tackling in real-time with commentary synced to the action
- **Rich broadcast experience** — play-by-play commentary with 400+ phrase fragments across 3 commentary styles, momentum tracking, drive narratives, and atmosphere text
- **Deep playbook system** — custom offensive/defensive plays, formation depth charts, tendencies/archetype system, and 13 down/distance buckets
- **Complete season lifecycle** — regular season, playoffs, offseason, draft, progression/regression, Hall of Fame, Ring of Honor
- **Primetime design system** — monochrome + accent color palette, glass panels, Outfit/DM Sans typography, page entrance choreography
- **Almanac** — 13-tab historical reference: awards, All-Pro, Hall of Fame, Ring of Honor, records, Top 10 career leaders, season leaders, recaps, history, past champions, franchise standings, draft history, and GM career
- **Confidence-based scouting** — 4-stage pipeline (School, Position, Targeted, Interviews) with pre-rolled 18-week college season across 50 schools and 5 conferences
- **Prose engine** — data-driven sports writing with vocabulary banks, context tagging, and variety tracking for professional-quality news articles
- **The Brandon Sams Show** — AI-generated sports radio with OpenAI TTS voices, caller personas, and auto-scrolling transcript sidebar
- **Narrative & immersion** — record-chase alerts, rivalry heat system, career milestones, multi-week story arcs (undefeated watch, dynasty, breakout players, revenge games)
- **SVG player portraits** — procedurally generated 11-layer faces with seeded RNG for every player, prospect, and Hall of Famer
- **ELO ratings & power rankings** — margin-of-victory adjusted ELO with tiered power rankings (Elite/Contender/Fringe/Rebuilding)
- **Live dashboard** — team overview, standings, playoff race, team leaders, weekly recap, injury report, upcoming games, recent schedule
- **Multiplayer** — multi-user leagues with per-game ready-up, live spectating, and commissioner tools

## Tech Stack

- React 19 + TypeScript + Vite 8
- Tailwind CSS v4 with Primetime design system
- Express API + SQLite backend
- Framer Motion animations
- OpenAI TTS + Cloudflare R2 (radio show)
- Deployed on Vercel (frontend) + Fly.io (backend)
