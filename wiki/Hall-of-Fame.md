# Hall of Fame

The Hall of Fame is the game'"'"'s era-relative legacy system.

It does not care only about raw totals. It cares about how dominant a player was compared with their era and peers.

## Hall Of Fame Rules

- Induction threshold: `175` legacy points
- Waiting period after retirement: `3` seasons
- Maximum inductees per year: `7`

That keeps the Hall from inflating too quickly.

## What Feeds The Score

- Seasonal rank finishes
- Awards
- Championships
- Longevity
- Career stats
- All-time rank placement

The exact formula is era-relative, which means a star from an older scoring environment is judged against the context they actually played in.

## Ring Of Honor

The Ring of Honor is the team-specific version of legacy recognition.

- Ring of Honor threshold: `85`
- Jersey retirement threshold: `130`
- Minimum team tenure: `4` seasons

That system rewards franchise identity, not just league-wide fame.

## Why It Matters

Hall of Fame and Ring of Honor are the game'"'"'s way of turning seasons into history. If you build a real dynasty, the game should remember it.
