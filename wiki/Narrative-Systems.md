# Narrative Systems

Narrative systems are the read-only story layer on top of simulation.

They do not change outcomes. They explain them.

## Record Chases

The game tracks when players are on pace to break records, when they are near a record, and when a record actually falls.

- Pace alerts start midseason.
- Near-record alerts show up later in the year.
- Career record chases can follow veterans across seasons.

## Milestones

Milestones cover both single-game explosions and long career arcs.

- Single-game milestones highlight big performances.
- Career milestones celebrate the long haul.

## Rivalries

Rivalry heat builds from:

- Close games
- Upsets
- Division games
- Playoff meetings

It decays in the offseason so old grudges do not clog the feed forever.

## Story Arcs

The long-running arcs include:

- Undefeated watch
- Dynasty run
- Breakout player
- Revenge game

These arcs are what make a season feel like more than a sequence of box scores.

## News And Radio

The story layer feeds the league news stream and the Brandon Sams Show radio feature. When the game says something big happened, the narrative systems are what decide how that moment gets framed.
