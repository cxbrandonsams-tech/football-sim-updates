# Football Sim

**The ultimate football franchise management simulation.**

Build your roster. Call the plays. Chase the championship.

---

## What You Get

Football Sim puts you in the GM's chair of a 32-team professional football league. Every decision matters: who to draft, who to cut, what plays to run, and how to build a dynasty that lasts.

### Manage Every Aspect of Your Franchise
- Scout college prospects through a 4-stage pipeline across an 18-week college season
- Draft, sign free agents, negotiate contracts, and manage your salary cap ($420M)
- Set your depth chart, design custom playbooks, and tune your team's tendencies
- Trade players and draft picks with AI-evaluated value matching

### Watch Your Strategy Play Out
- **Play-by-play simulation** resolves every snap through realistic 5-phase pass and run engines
- **2D animated visualization** shows all 22 players running routes, blocking, and covering in real-time
- **400+ commentary phrases** across 3 styles sync to the action as it happens
- Speed control (1x / 2x / 4x) so you can watch every detail or blitz through

### Deep Season Lifecycle
Regular season (18 weeks) &rarr; Playoffs (NFL-style bracket) &rarr; Offseason (free agency, contract demands) &rarr; Draft &rarr; repeat. Progression and regression keep your roster evolving. Every season matters.

### Rich Historical Record
- **13-tab Almanac**: Awards, All-Pro, Hall of Fame, Ring of Honor, career records, Top 10 all-time leaders, season leaders, recaps, history, past champions, franchise standings, draft history, GM career
- **ELO ratings** and tiered power rankings track how teams stack up
- **Narrative systems** generate record-chase alerts, rivalry storylines, dynasty watches, and breakout player arcs

### Premium Radio Experience
*The Brandon Sams Show* is an AI-generated sports radio program with full audio production. Five distinct voice characters debate your league's storylines, react to trades, preview the draft, and call out your worst moves. Episodes trigger automatically at key moments throughout the season.

### Built for the Eye
The **Primetime** design system delivers a premium dark-mode experience with monochrome surfaces, 6 accent colors, glass panel effects, and staggered page animations. Every player has a unique procedurally generated SVG portrait. Custom team logos for all 32 franchises.
