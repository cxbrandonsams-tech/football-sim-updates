# Coaching

Coaching is a real part of the roster layer, not just flavor text.

## Coach Roles

- Head coaches run the team.
- Offensive coordinators shape offense-specific scheme and playcalling.
- Defensive coordinators shape defense-specific scheme and playcalling.

## Coach Archetypes

The coach pool uses five archetypes:

- Developer
- Strategist
- Motivator
- Recruiter
- Disciplinarian

Those archetypes influence how the AI thinks about a coach and how a coach fits a roster.

## Star Ratings

- Head coaches use 1-3 stars.
- Coordinators use 1-2 stars.
- Better coaches have stronger playcalling and better upside.

## Offseason Flow

- Offseason week 1: you can fire a coach after elimination or during the opening window.
- Offseason week 2: the coach market opens.
- Offseason week 2 and later: you can hire from the market.

## Promotions And Poaching

- If your team has no head coach, you can promote a coordinator.
- Coordinators can be poached from strong teams if the timing and conditions line up.
- A promoted coordinator keeps the coach tree moving for the team that lost them.

## Progression And Retirement

- Coaches can improve after good seasons.
- Bad seasons can knock them down a tier.
- Coaches eventually retire, which keeps the coaching market moving over long franchise saves.

## Why It Matters

A good roster can still underperform if the coaching fit is bad. Coaching is one of the main ways the game gives personality to a franchise over time.
