# Roadmap

Planned features and future direction for Football Sim. Items are roughly in priority order but subject to change.

---

## Near Term

### Almanac
A comprehensive historical reference hub with sub-pages:
- All-Time Records (single-game, single-season, career)
- Top 10 All-Time stat leaderboards
- Season Recaps (year-by-year summaries)
- All-Pro Selections by season
- Season Leaders by category
- Award History (MVP, OPOY, DPOY, OROY, DROY, etc.)
- Past Champions with bracket results
- Past Draft History (round-by-round picks)
- All-Time Franchise Standings

### Trade Block
- List players available for trade
- AI-driven trade market with interest indicators
- Trade block visible on dashboard

### Waiver Wire
- Claim recently released players
- Priority-based waiver order
- Recent waiver activity feed

### Team Logo Optimization
- Re-export team logos as 256x256 square PNGs (currently oversized landscape images)

---

## Medium Term

### Enhanced 2D Visualization
- More route variety and defensive scheme animations
- Instant replay for key plays
- Camera zoom on big plays

### Multiplayer Enhancements
- Improved multi-user league management
- Commissioner tools expansion
- Trade negotiation between human GMs

### Mobile Responsive Design
- Touch-friendly controls
- Responsive layout for tablets and phones

---

## Long Term

### Advanced AI
- Smarter CPU team management (trades, free agency, draft)
- Coaching personality impact on game simulation

### Expanded Game Modes
- Fantasy draft leagues
