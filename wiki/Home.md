# Football Sim

**The football franchise management sim where roster building, scheme fit, and season history all matter.**

Build your roster. Call the plays. Chase the championship.

---

## What You Get

Football Sim puts you in the GM chair of a 32-team professional football league. Every decision matters: who to draft, who to cut, what plays to call, and how to build a dynasty that lasts.

### Manage Every Part Of The Franchise

- Scout college prospects through a 4-stage draft pipeline
- Scout individual players and watch rating noise shrink as scouting improves
- Draft, sign free agents, negotiate contracts, and manage a $420M salary cap
- Handle waivers, the trade block, coaching moves, and fifth-year options

### Watch The Strategy Play Out

- Play-by-play simulation resolves every snap through explicit pass and run phases
- 2D animation shows all 22 players running routes, blocking, covering, and tackling in real-time
- Commentary and radio react to what happens on the field
- Speed control lets you watch slowly or blaze through a week

### Build Across Seasons

- Regular season, playoffs, offseason, draft, and preseason all feed into each other
- ELO and power rankings track team strength over time
- Narrative systems track records, rivalries, milestones, and long arcs
- The Almanac preserves the permanent record of your league

### Premium Broadcast Flavor

*The Brandon Sams Show* is the league radio show. It reacts to your season, your trades, your injuries, and your big moments with a full audio presentation.

### Built For The Eye

The Primetime visual system keeps the presentation premium: monochrome surfaces, sharp accents, glass treatment, and seeded player portraits.
