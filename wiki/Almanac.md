# Almanac

The Almanac is the long-term memory of the league.

It stores the stuff that should still matter after the current week is over.

## Current History Stack

- Hall of Fame
- Ring of Honor
- Records
- Season Recaps
- Head to Head
- Replays
- GM Career

Hall of Fame lives as its own top-level history page, while the rest of the Almanac tabs sit together in the history section.

## What It Shows

- League history by season
- All-time player and team records
- Franchise-level standing summaries
- Preserved game replays
- Your GM career if you are in a save with that mode enabled

## Replays

The replay archive keeps notable games even after ordinary logs age out:

- Playoff games
- Human-vs-human games
- Upsets
- Record or newsworthy games

That way the league still has a memory of the games that mattered.

## Why It Matters

The Almanac is where the game turns history into a feature. It gives your franchise a permanent archive instead of forcing you to remember everything yourself.
