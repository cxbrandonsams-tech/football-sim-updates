# Season Lifecycle

Every season in Football Sim follows a complete lifecycle that mirrors the real NFL calendar. Each phase brings different decisions and challenges.

---

## Regular Season (18 Weeks)

The core gameplay loop. Each week:

1. **Set your gameplan** — adjust play-calling by down/distance, tweak philosophy sliders
2. **Manage your roster** — check injuries, adjust depth chart, consider waiver claims
3. **Play or sim your game** — watch it live with the 2D visualization, or simulate instantly
4. **Read the news** — game recaps, trade rumors, record chases, rivalry updates, narrative arcs
5. **Advance the week** — the league progresses, other games resolve, injuries update

### During the season:
- **Scouting stages** run on deadlines (stages 1-3 overlap with regular season weeks)
- **Waiver wire** — cut players hit waivers for 1 week; claim order is reverse-standings before week 10, then rolling
- **Trade block** — list players as available, see AI interest
- **Trading** — propose and respond to trades at any time
- **College season** — one college week revealed per NFL week (scouting intel drips in)

## Playoffs (NFL-Style Bracket)

- 7 teams per conference qualify (division winners + wild cards)
- Wild Card &rarr; Divisional &rarr; Conference Championship &rarr; League Championship
- Higher seeds get home advantage (reflected in ELO calculations)
- **Stage 4 interviews** run during playoffs — your last chance to deep-scout before the draft

## Offseason

After the championship:

- **Contract demands** — players with expiring contracts or dissatisfaction make salary demands
- **Free agency** — released and expired-contract players enter the market
- **Extend or cut** — decide which players to keep at what price
- **Progression/regression** — players age, develop, or decline based on development trait and age curves
- **Awards** — MVP, Offensive/Defensive Player of the Year, Rookie of the Year, Comeback Player
- **All-Pro selections** — 1st and 2nd team by position
- **Hall of Fame inductions** — eligible retired players evaluated (175-point threshold, 3-season wait, max 7 per year)
- **Ring of Honor** — per-franchise honors for loyal franchise greats (85-point threshold, 4-season tenure minimum)

## Draft

- Multi-round draft with all 32 teams
- Your scouting confidence determines how well you know each prospect
- Pick-by-pick or auto-sim options
- Drafted players join your roster with their true ratings revealed
- After the draft completes: advance to start the next season

## The Long Game

Across seasons, your franchise builds history:

- **ELO rating** tracks your team's strength over time
- **Power rankings** shift week-to-week
- **Rivalry heat** accumulates between division foes and playoff adversaries
- **Narrative arcs** track undefeated runs, dynasty quests, breakout players, and revenge storylines
- **The Almanac** records everything — every champion, every award winner, every record, every draft pick
- **GM Career** tracks your personal legacy across all your seasons

The game rewards patience and long-term thinking. A dynasty isn't built in one draft.
